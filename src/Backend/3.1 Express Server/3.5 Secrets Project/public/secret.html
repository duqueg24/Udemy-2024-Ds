Secrets

  • When making chocolate desserts, always add a little salt. It makes the chocolate flavor more intense.
  • Whenever you travel abroad, bring a new soundtrack for each place you visit, preferably one you have never heard before. In the future, each soundtrack will evoke vivid memories of the places you have visited.
  • Do not try to be the person your parents want you to be. Be the person you’d like your child to be. This clearly defines your own convictions, desires, and goals, and motivates you to be your best.
  • If a friend or family member is diagnosed with dementia or Alzheimer’s, in the early stages, find out their favorite songs. This way, you can create a playlist that may be of great benefit in later stages.
  • When a friend is upset, ask them a simple question before saying anything else: “Do you want to talk about it, or do you want to be distracted from it?”
  • If you forget your WiFi password, or want to retrieve your school’s WiFi password, enter this command in a connected computer’s terminal: netsh wlan show profile WiFi-name key=clear
